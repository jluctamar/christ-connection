Mission Statement
Our mission is to Delve deeper into God's Word.
Discussing and Dissecting His scriptures to gain a better understanding of Him.
As we continue to Digest the bread of life, we strive to apply His wisdom in our everyday lives.
Continually Developing spiritually and growing as Disciples Christ.

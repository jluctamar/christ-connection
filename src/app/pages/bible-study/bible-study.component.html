Current Lesson: Jesus and Zacchaeus (Luke 19: 1-10)

God calls us to express our repentance through exuburant generosity. This is made clear when Jesus compares the kingdom of God as a treasure found in a field, so precious that the man who finds it is willing to give all he has to keep it (Matthew 13:44). Or consider Jesus' exhortation in Matthew 10: 5-8 "freely as you have recieved, freely give". The main point is simply this "store up your treasures in heaven" as opposed to chasing after earthly treasures that will fade away. The focus of our current study is on Zacchaeus, a man who came to understand this kingdom truth well. Join us as we continue to Delve deeper into the scriptures and learn from the story of Zacchaeus the preciousness of Gods' grace.
